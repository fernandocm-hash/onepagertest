What winners are building now
01
Continuous discovery, not periodic research

Leading teams track real-time signals: product momentum, hiring velocity, customer wins, and tech shifts. By the time traditional sourcing finds a target, signal-led teams have already built relationships.

02
Coverage beats volume

60% comprehensive market coverage is more valuable than 100% superficial knowledge. Winners understand breadth, depth, and freshness — not just headcount of companies tracked.

03
Cross-functional alignment

Innovation and Corp Dev operating separately creates duplicated work, missed opportunities, and internal friction. Winners integrate these teams with shared tools, processes, and success metrics.
